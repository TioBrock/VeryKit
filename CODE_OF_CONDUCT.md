# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

VeryKit is an open source project for QA Engineers, developers, students, and technical communities.

We want this community to be welcoming, respectful, and productive for people with different backgrounds, experience levels, identities, and perspectives.

Contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to help create a space where technical discussion can happen with clarity and care.

## Expected Behavior

Expected behavior includes:

- Being respectful and considerate.
- Giving constructive feedback.
- Welcoming newcomers.
- Asking questions in good faith.
- Explaining decisions clearly.
- Focusing criticism on ideas, code, and outcomes rather than people.
- Respecting different experience levels.
- Assuming good intent while still addressing harmful behavior directly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats.
- Insults, slurs, or demeaning language.
- Personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Discrimination based on identity, background, or personal characteristics.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of discussions.
- Trolling or bad-faith participation.
- Dismissing accessibility, inclusion, or safety concerns.

## Technical Disagreement

Technical disagreement is welcome when handled respectfully.

Healthy disagreement should:

- Stay focused on the problem.
- Use evidence where possible.
- Clarify tradeoffs.
- Separate preferences from requirements.
- Leave room for maintainers to make final decisions.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior.
- Responding to reports.
- Taking appropriate action when this code is violated.
- Applying standards consistently and fairly.
- Protecting contributors from harassment or abuse.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers.

If the issue is security-related, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.

When reporting conduct concerns, include:

- What happened.
- Where it happened.
- Who was involved.
- Any relevant links or screenshots.
- Whether immediate action is needed.

Reports will be handled with care and discretion.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all VeryKit project spaces, including:

- Issues.
- Pull requests.
- Discussions.
- Documentation.
- Community channels connected to the project.
- Maintainer interactions related to the project.

It also applies when someone represents the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Possible enforcement actions include:

- Private clarification.
- Public reminder.
- Comment or issue moderation.
- Temporary participation restriction.
- Permanent participation restriction.

The response should match the severity and context of the behavior.

## Shared Goal

VeryKit exists to help people work faster and better. A healthy community is part of that mission.
